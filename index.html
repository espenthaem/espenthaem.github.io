'Bombay Dreaming' - an artificial intelligence based art installation completed as part of a residency at brahman_ai. The installation combines the art in Bombay Beach, with the faces of the resident community to create dreams that capture the complex relationship between artists and their environment. As the sun sets, the installation comes to life at Brahman's remote-desert location, Mars, ready to be stumbled upon.

Based on StyleTransfer, a Deep Convolutional Autoencoder and open-cv.
